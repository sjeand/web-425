Paul Simon




Paul Simon was born in October of 1941. Paul Simon was born in New Jersey but later moved to the big apple. Here, he met Art Garfunkel. The two became fast friends but their musical career was just as fast. Art Garfunkel left for art school and Paul Simon headed to college. While attending college he continued to write music. Later, the dup was reunited and with Garfunkel’s voice and Simon’s composing abilities, their music career finally took off!
