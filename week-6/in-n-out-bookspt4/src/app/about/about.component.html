Anna Dewdney

Anna Dewdney was the author and illustrator of the children’s book series, Llama Llama. She was born in New York City in 1965. Living in New York, she worked as a mail carrier and a teacher at a boy boarding school. Until she decided to pursue her dreams and become an author. Dewdney felt strongly about children’s literacy and wrote her books to encourage reading and to help children through every day situations that cause anxiety. Dewdney lost her battle with cancer in 2016 leaving behind her legacy.
